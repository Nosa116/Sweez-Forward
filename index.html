Join The Team

Sweez Forward 2025 is on the hunt for bold, visionary leaders to join the Core Team. If you’re passionate about empowering young professionals, driven to make real impact, and ready to lead a high-stakes, high-reward initiative—this is your call.

As a Core Team member, you won’t just help plan an event—you’ll own major parts of it. You’ll lead teams, shape strategy, and steer execution for one of the most ambitious, high-impact career events of the year. From ideation to event day, you’ll be at the center of it all.


We are looking for:

  • Visionaries who can think big and execute even bigger
  • Leaders who inspire and motivate teams to achieve greatness
  • Strategic thinkers who can navigate challenges with creativity and resilience
  • Passionate advocates for youth empowerment and career development

This isn’t just a volunteer role—it’s a leadership opportunity with real responsibility and impact. Let’s build something unforgettable. Apply now to join the Core Team.

Sweez Forward 2025 is on the hunt for bold, visionary leaders to join the Core Team. If you’re passionate about empowering young professionals, driven to make real impact, and ready to lead a high-stakes, high-reward initiative—this is your call.

This isn’t just a volunteer role—it’s a leadership opportunity with real responsibility and impact. Let’s build something unforgettable. Apply now to join the Core Team.
